Andy Lane

Video Editing

ANDY LANE is a film maker who has amassed a broad range of skills and experience in a range of media. An experienced camera man, editor, director and writer, Andy honed his skills making short films, music video clips, and corporate video. He has recently finished writing and directing two short films and is currently developing a TV series for Reelhead Productions.
