Vanitas

Hui Wai Keung

Now we might not pray to the ancient deities, however, whenever we encounter the extraterrestrial beings, human are still fatigue. We still cannot escape from the inevitable cycle of earthly life. At the same time, we keep wondering. We wish to evolve to a new form of life. We desire for immortality and eternity. Thus, I have chosen worship as my way of communication, in the codec of the 17th century still life paintings. Vanity of vanities, all is vanity.

Hui Wai Keung was born and lives in Hong Kong. He received his MFA from School of Creative Media, City University of Hong Kong, and graduated in diploma in Fine Art (Sculpture) from Hong Kong Art School. In recent years, Hui has been active in artist-in-residency programs, held in Europe and Asia. He had participated in exhibitions in Hong Kong, Korea, Argentina, Greece, and so on. He believes that the power of art can create a new universe with alternative possibility. It can help escaping from the ordinary life.
