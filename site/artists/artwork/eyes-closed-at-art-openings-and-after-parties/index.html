Eyes Closed at Art Openings and After-Parties

Nat Thomas

Portraits of people with their eyes partially closed are deemed worthless by conventional editorial parameters. Launching a gold record of artist contributions into space is beyond conventional curatorial parameters, making eyes closed at art openings and after parties a match that could be best explored beyond the heavens. An esoteric original sound composition has been provided by my 10 year old daughter Maxine.
