4.57 x 10⁹

S U N N S T E D E

As a composer, I am deeply convinced that in every little sound our world has to offer, a symphony is hidden. It’s up to us to lend an ear and decipher these everchanging scores, the same way we pick apples from the tree. It’s a gift from Nature, thus a gift from the Universe. — Following that philosophy, « 4.57 x 10⁹ » acts as an earthling soundscape manifesto by mixing raw & primitive elements (birds songs, water & snow noises, …) with contemporary elements (transformed & manipulated guitar takes, apparatus noises, …). — « Hello, whoever you are. Those are the kind of sounds and noises that can be heard on our planet… »

Sound, noise, music — creating with everything because everything is creative. — Sunnstede is a one-man experimental musical project. — Sunnstede is organic and synthetic, implosive and explosive, raw and transformed, structured and amorphous, electric and acoustic, perpetual and volatile.
