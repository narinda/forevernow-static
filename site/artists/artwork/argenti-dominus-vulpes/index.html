"ARGENTI DOMINUS VULPES"

EMILIANO MAGGI

Selected recording on tapes of foxes mating season. Recorded in Italy and England , between February and March 2012.

Emiliano Maggi
Lives and works in Rome, Italy.

Inside the work of Emiliano Maggi there is a strong dialogue between simbolism, mythology, popular iconography and ancestral nature . The ancient yearning mystery of the rural world: the animals metaphor that tells the thrill of the individual struck by the discovery of the absolute nature; a world where another reality is possible, where the “non verbal instinct” is made and expressed without fear. The artist use different technics and media from perfomance, sound installations, sculptures, photography and video.

His works has been exhibited at : The Nymph song – Mona Museum, Hobart -Tasmania 2014 Subway Dreams – Art Book Fair – MomA PS1 – New York 2013 Vision & Prayer – Lorcan O'Neill Gallery , Rome 2013 Les interimettences du coeur – Ex Elettrofonica, Rome 2012 Re-Generation – Macro Testaccio – Rome 2012 Moon Ra – Victoria & Albert Museum, London 2011 Patria Interiore –Golden Threat Gallery –Belfast 2011 Twin mind- Spazio Carbonesi – Artefieraoff – Bologna 2011 Argenti Dominus Vulpes – Rongwrongallery – Amsterdam 2011 Argenti Dominus Vulpes – Artissima Lido –Torino 2011 Premio LUM- Teatro Margherita – Bari 2011 Argenti Dominus Vulpes – Mambo Bologna 2011 Argenti Dominus Vulpes – Brownprojectspace- Milan 2011 When in Rome -Italian Culture Institute- Los Angeles 2011 Rituals – Area 63 –Rome 2010 Life is Art-Santa Rosa-California 2010 Nomas Foundation –Rome 2010 The Swiss Culture Institute in Rome.IT-2010 The Yuko Sueta Art Auction-NY.USA- 2009 Voodoo Fest:Art & Music 2009-New Orleans,La.USA Arabesque by Archivial Editions at the Chinati Foundation Marfa.TX.
