Images Reconstruction

Abshar Platisza

imagination is the most beautiful gift of god.

I often go alone away from the city where I live, sometimes I just walk, sit, and hear what is there. in certain circumstances my head often respond to what I hear, and display a visual and events in my head. and sometimes I often record sound in the atmosphere where I was sitting. Because sometimes I have always wanted to be in a place far, it sounds to me is a way to recall memories.

This work is a reconstruction of what I heard in the distance the city and imaginative events in my head.

Human imagination should be infinite, and humans have a variety of ways to make it real

Abshar Platisza is an artist who work and lived in Indonesia. He was born January 5, 1992. majoring in fine arts, concentrating in printmaking and drawing medium.
