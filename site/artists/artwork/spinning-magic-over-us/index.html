Spinning Magic Over Us

Violeta Lisboa

1 min clip of a durational piece named Spinning Magic Over Us, executed on a roof top in Lisbon in the summer of 2012.

Violeta Lisboa creates interdisciplinary works, embrcing the mediums of dance, physical theatre, live art, music, performance, film and instalation. She focuses on the subconscious, the world of dreams, symbols and arquetypes and is a strong believer of the artist as a modern shaman with the duty of transforming minds and ideas in our contemporary society.
