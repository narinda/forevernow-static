Perpetual Motion

Clare St Clare, Dru Blumenshied, Daniel and Gideon Frankel & The Bumesi Collective

Illumination Her tears are golden Her tears are sunlight Is she the queen or is she the fool? Perpetual motion and she smiles Perpetual motion and she flies Perpetual Motion portrays the positive and negative elements to women in humanity as we know it and how it could be in the future if we continue to progress as we live now. Visual themes included in this film are the freedom of female expression, beauty and the body, emotional imprisonment, non humanistic influence and judgements, the negative social influence on women’s choices on how to live as our world shifts towards the world of the machine.

The soundtrack includes a female voice, bee’s drone recorded from live swarm, jupiter 8 Sine Wave, King David Harp and tuned to Solfeggio frequency 528hz.

Music by St Clare, Gideon & Daniel Frankel Directed by Dru Blumensheid St Clare Lorna Deng Dru Blumensheid Costume Design by Bumesi Hair & Makeup by Nadira Shamid Gaffer Assistant Mariah Sliwczynski Shot in Melbourne, Australia, 2014

Clare St Clare is a multi media performance artist. She creates work inspired by dreams, beauty and fragility in nature, art, philosophy and science.

Gideon and Daniel Frankel are multi award winning Australian music producers and multi instrumentalists.

Dru Blumensheid is an Australian-American video and performance artist. She works in mediums of fashion and textile design, sculpture, new media art, dance and photography with her international collaborative crew called The Bumesi Collective.
