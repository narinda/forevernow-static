PAIX BINAIRE/BINARY PEACE

NINA KARDEC

I translated into bianire language the word “peace.” I wanted to say with my human voice representing our species. I’ve also included the songs of birds recorded in the countryside with my cell phone. I wanted the natural element be included. In addition, they are perfectly rhythmic as voice and instruments. The music is electronic but also human. The rhythm is life. stagnation is death.

Born in 1970, live an work in Paris (France/Europe) Djiing begins in 2000 until today in 2 formats: MK2 turntables and laptop. Plays in bars, clubs and festivals in France and Europe. her Musical composition started in 2006. Artistic collaborations/ compilations or albums with netlabels French and Scots (uk) in 2007 (Section27/Vaatican records / Creative Obsession/Pavillon36). Nina Kardec also remixes of other artists (Milanese / Syndrom / Pollux / Nikola Grebovic-Scalameriya ..). Compositions Nina Kardec and outlets are mostly related to Nature, but always with the rhythm of life, or dark and introspective, or solar. His latest album is free to download via Vaatican Records.

Nina watch the society in a stratospheric point of view, and retranscrie music, his human perspective.
