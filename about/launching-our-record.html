FOREVER NOW LAUNCHES

We love to launch, the website, the record and of course into space.

RECORD LAUNCH, MONA FOMA FESTIVAL, HOBART TASMANIA

The Forever Now record was launched on Sunday 18th January 2015 with a live transmission into outer space. It was a wild party with importantly a reveal of the golden record itself and the incredible works of the artists from the website.

There was live performances from Le Gateau Chocolat, Atlanta Eke, Gotye & Amanda Palmer and an incredible bevy of local dancers from Hobart transformed into slime creatures from outer space for the event.

WEBSITE LAUNCH

The Forever Now website was launched in at the 2013 International Symposium of Electronic Art at a special event at the State Library of Victoria.

We are making a point of speaking about the project at as many places as humanly possible so far this has included: the Kosmica Conference at the prestigious Labatorio Arte Alameda, Mexico City; Linden Centre for Contemporary Art, Melbourne as part of their Space Oddity exhibition and Kings ARI, Melbourne.

LAUNCHING INTO SPACE The Forever Now record is an object and we intend this object to be launched into space. We are currently in conversation with private and public space enterprises to make this happen.
