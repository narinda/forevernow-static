FIRE MISHAP

SHIVKUMAR K V

Everything happens or anything, Consciousness matters is men’s way of life. Putting fire may not be a big job but without consciousness, people often fire the unwanted items may be grass, Dry leafs, paper, plastic or any item beneath the shadow giving and life giving trees. This is a not a small mistake but a big mishap which may cause the huge fall of environmental needs. Need to plant a tree when one we destroy, If not at least we preserve for the future. Human, Flora and fauna or the ecology are served by nature and mother earth. Going Green might free us from the any kind threat. A bit consciousness needed for the greenly growth.
