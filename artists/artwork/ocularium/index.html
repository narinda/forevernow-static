Ocularium

Ioan Colbeanu (Pupila)

‘Ocularium’ is an AV collage of vivid dreams in natural and virtual landscapes. It explores a glimpse of perception from a ‘human terrarium’, that might also be seen as a micro-dimension of planet Terra.

‘Ocularium’ AV collage is shot in Aarhus, Denmark, during one year of random exploration in nature, and is inspired by Tony Oursler’s ‘Face to face’ installation, exhibited at AROS Museum. The soundtrack for ‘Ocularium’ is called ‘Second coming’ and is produced by Nava Spatiala (romanian name for'The Spacesheep') – https://soundcloud.com/nava_spatiala

~see you is space~

Self-taught artist, born in Romania, 1982. Exhibitions> ‘Skin Ego’, Saint Ink Bucharest, 2011 (scan/print), ‘Eartquake’s coming’ (photo / public intervention), Carol 53, Bucharest 2012, ‘Facebook Swastik 2.0’ – Blaue Fabrik, Dresden, 2013 (painting, illustration). See full works on blog.
