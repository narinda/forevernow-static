On Death

Gabrielle de Vietri

As part of the Philosophy for Kids (2009) video interview project, a group of children aged 7-10 years old share their thoughts on life, death and everything in between. This 1 minute excerpt focuses on their responses to questions about death.

Project: Gabrielle de Vietri Interviewer: Margaret Paul Interviewees: Marcel Mena and Pia Day Wilson Camera: Nick Spunde Set: Nigel Guy and Colin Forbes-Abrams

This work was originally commissioned by the Gallery of Modern Art / Queensland Art Gallery and the Edinburgh International Festival 2009

Gabrielle de Vietri is an artist who works across many media including video, performance, sculpture, text, events and images. Her work often explores non-verbal and verbal communication, especially the ways in which codes of language are used, interpreted and manipulated to create or distort meaning.

Gabrielle completed a Master of Fine Arts at Monash University in 2013, and a BFA (Honours) at VCA.
