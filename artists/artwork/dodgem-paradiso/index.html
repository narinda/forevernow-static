Dodgem Paradiso

Danielle Zorbas

Dodgem Paradiso projects in open dimensions flooded with then emptied of memory, driven by diegetic sound: the bump and whir of noise in the signal.

Danielle is a video artist/phd candidate exploring fluid identity, surfaces and other dimensions.
