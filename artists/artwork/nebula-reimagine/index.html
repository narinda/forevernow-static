Nebula/Reimagine

Antriksh Bali

I wrote this song as a part of my independently self-released debut EP ‘Reimaginations from A Different Universe’.

It tries to artistically combine elements of orchestral music with bits and pieces of electronic music, not just because it seemed to suit the mood of the entire album in particular but also because it tries to bring together the chaotic (electronic) and calm (orchestral) parts of the universe we live in.

The mood of the track is supposed to promote wonder and curiosity in the sense of an awakening of the power of the human imagination which has no limits or boundaries, the same sense of wonder that makes human beings reach out to the stars and discover new things about the universe, both outside and inside within us.

Antriksh Bali is an independent Alternative/Electronic musician based in New Delhi, India. Raised and brought up in an environment where there were no restrictions on genre or styles, Antriksh played and mixed up various styles of music constantly learning and experimenting with sounds to arrive at a trademark sound that combines various elements of electronic and orchestral music.

Learning western classical piano since the age of 11, He has roots in classical music which he skilfully combines with elements of modern music that encapsulate everything from Electronica, Trip-Hop and Ambient to Industrial to create music with a vibe that is both unique and unseen.
