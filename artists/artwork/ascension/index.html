Ascension

Grayson Cooke

This is an image of Jesus Christ – a popular religious figure on Earth – ascending via a fiery crucible to the inky blackness of the heavens. Or maybe it’s not an image at all – maybe it’s an exercise in elemental form, maybe it is time-lapse video of a random 35mm film negative dissolving gradually in sulphuric acid…

Grayson Cooke is an inter-disciplinary scholar and media artist
