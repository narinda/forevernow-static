Be Drop, Be Rain, Be Ice, Be Ocean.wav

Shycacti

I think, If something could be sent to outer space, It must is totally the most important thing for us , and for earth too. It’s water. It make our planet was called Blue Star. I lived in Taiwan, a small island arms of the Pacific Ocean. We can go to sea from anywhere of the island. I love her, respect her, care her, guard her. So I wanna sent a song to outer space for her.

musician, artist. One-man experimental sound project, in between sound and music, dedicating to freely explore the function of them, in order to give the multi-layers sensational movement for listening. type: ambient/electronic www.facebook.com/shycacti
