not modern art

ishita gupta

dear reader , after I saw some other entries for this , I realized what they might be asking for . modern art. my video is far from that it is conventional in every form possible it doesn’t require anyone to use they’re grey cells , my message is so clear it’s literally been typed in . so now to come to the point about why I might have uploaded this dear reader . it is because drumroll well I was asked to describe the world and truthfully the world is quite conventional right now . I decided to describe what is probably considered most important by billions of people across the globe . So important that some could kill others for it and be married in its name , at the same time . Religion , I believe that all problems good and bad have their source from it . from the pope to Allah , we all (well nearly all ) have our beliefs and deep down inside we know how much it means . it is imperative that we describe this funny phenomenon of how our race tries to come up with reasons about our existence . thank you for reading this dear user ,it means a lot to the stars . :) (be happy )

I am a student of Sanskriti school ,in new delhi . I am currently studying in ninth grade .however , I have a hobby of making stop motion films . I have made 5 so far , they’ve been recognized for awards many times . last year for BAFTA faith shorts and most recently for the international children’s international film festival . I love the universe and cats .
