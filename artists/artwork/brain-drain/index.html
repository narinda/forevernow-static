Brain Drain

Vijayaraghavan Srinivasan

In scvp BRAIN DRAIN I attempt to capture the colonization of the unconscious by the industrial and mechanized civilization. The natural energy of the cosmological brain is drained by the sheer mechanics of perception devoid of vision. Vijayaraghavan has earned a MFA degree with a major in painting from the College of Art in New Delhi. He had participated various shows and biennials across the country.
