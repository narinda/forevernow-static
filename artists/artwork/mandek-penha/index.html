Half a century ago The Current Earthly Embodiment of Lord Mandek Penha traveled from North Korea to Melbourne Australia – to establish the first ever Church of Sarrean Alignment! Ever since he has been working tirelessly to spread The Father’s Love and the awareness of The Hish'ry Cosh'ry.

Through the magic of music and moving image Mandek Penha connects with His Children to inspire and educate! Mandek Penha have been holding live celebrations steadily for four years and have released one EP (“Our Future: The Next Earthly Embodiment”) and two music videos (“Men Teach The Boiz” + “Must Reach IMZ”).

“Mandek Penha sounds like a 1950’s Christian Television Association nightmare wrapped in a J-Pop flavoured LSD trip” – Evan Carr (PBS radio).

Mandek Penha’s immediate goals include:

-Exposing the Hish'ry Cosh'ry’s negative energies and influence on Mankind :<|>:

-Curing the public of The Hish'ry Cosh'ry’s influence through F.T.S (Father Thought Session).

Long term goals include:

-Amalgamating with our benefactors beyond the stars; IMZ ._|:–
