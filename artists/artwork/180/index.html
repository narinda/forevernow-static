180

Zlatko Cosic

Each video within “180” was originally created for three different sets of the 60x60 Project, which is comprised of Cosic’s 60 videos with 60 second compositions by 60 different composers. These one hundred eighty videos form a new visual structure which inspired the creation of sixty seconds of sound to accompany the final audio visual experience.

Zlatko Cosic is a video artist born in the former Yugoslavia, now living in St. Louis, USA. His work involves creating experimental and documentary art, including short films, installations, public and theater projections, and live video performances. The visuals and sounds are often related to direct reality, and he attempts to bridge the concrete and abstract and explore real stories in experimental ways. In the process of creating an art piece, he alters visuals and experiments with the form and structure. By adding abstraction and implementing sounds, he constructs another level of aesthetic experience in his video art.
