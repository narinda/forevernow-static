Ascent

Mike Estabrook

Ascent is a short animation depicting the aging of a single young man.

Mike Estabrook is a Brooklyn based artist who has exhibited in group exhibitions internationally, and has recently done solo projects at Mixed Greens, Bliss on Bliss Art Projects, Art in Odd Places, and the Krannert museum of art. He has held residencies at both the LMCC Swing Space and Workspace, NY Arts Beijing, and the MacDowelL Colony.
