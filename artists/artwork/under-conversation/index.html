Under Conversation

Ankit K. Patel

In this work contradictions can be seen, read and heard due to the three languages- visual, verbal and text. We humans communicate with other, by exchange of knowledge, beliefs, opinions, wishes, threats, commands, thanks, promises, feelings but its only our individual imagination that falls apart.

Ankit Patel’s work is influenced by her past experiences. His inspirational was his father Kiran Patel as an designer and photographer. His short stature allowing him to satirically react at thing which he see around him, as far as technique is concerned, it goes back to where he come from, observing his father’s daily photography practice and experimenting with this medium in his work. The final visuals turn up being portrayed on a humorous side.
