Bubble Etc.

Youngshin Jeon

For this work Youngshin Jeon interweaves past, present and future and explores how to vanish on the timeline nevertheless linked by certain inevitability.

Youngshin’s work explores the way we understand the present trough various way. Always diverse, her subjects have included the religious, personal life, universal, and history. She is studying BA in fine Art at Goldsmiths, with evident intrigue in ecological term of art through her various works. She contemplated communication on the network through performance work using Face book and taking part in musical collaborative art project, ‘Four room’.

2014 DEL+REW, Online Platform, London, UK Link: http://www.delrew.com

2014 Symposium : A Banquet of Korean Contemporary Art, Barge house, Oxo tower Wharf, London, UK Link: http://www.4482.info
