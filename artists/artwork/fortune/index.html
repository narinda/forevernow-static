Vandana Jain

FORTUNE features the top 100 global companies as rated by Fortune magazine in 2011. The Walmart starburst, Pemex eagle, and IBM logotype appear slowly on screen in order of rank, accompanied by a variety of open-source sounds, ranging from the industrial to the spiritual to the natural.

Vandana Jain is a visual artist and textile designer based in Brooklyn, NY. Jain has exhibited widely in the United States as well as abroad. Through sculpture, painting, embroidery, audio and animation, she recontextualizes the rich visual symbolic language surrounding us to comment on capitalism, globalization and consumerism.
